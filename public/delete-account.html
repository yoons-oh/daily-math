🗑️ Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your MathMagic account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

What will be deleted

  • Your login account (email & password)
  • Subscription information
  • Push notification settings
⚠️ Note: Learning progress, profiles, rewards, and streaks are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will remove this local data automatically.

How to request deletion

Send an email to ka2087@naver.com with:

• Subject: Account Deletion Request – MathMagic
• Your registered email address

We will delete your account within 7 business days and send a confirmation email.
